Small Business Cloud Adoption Case Studies

See how other small businesses restructured their operations, saved capital, and boosted team output using tailored cloud-based tools.

The Daily Olive Deli

Profile: Specialty gourmet deli with three physical shop locations.

Before Cloud:

Used hand-written daybooks and manual spreadsheet updates for stock counts once a week, leading to frequent inventory shortages on high-margin ingredients.

After Cloud (SaaS integration):

Transitioned to Shopify POS synced with a unified QuickBooks Online catalog. Real-time automatic inventory tracking across all storefronts.

+24% Sales Revenue

from eliminated out-of-stock events & live stock monitoring

M&R Legal Advisory

Profile: Local real estate law and conveyancing partnership with 12 associates.

Before Cloud:

Local NAS drive server kept in-office. Slow VPN connectivity for remote staff led to duplicate drafts and document version confusion.

After Cloud (SaaS Productivity):

Adopted Microsoft 365 SharePoint Online with unified co-authoring tools and standardized remote client portals.

-14 hours/week

saved per staff member on contract draft reviews

Apex Steel Works

Profile: Independent steel frame fabrication workshop, 30 workers.

Before Cloud:

CAD blueprint files stored on individual hard drives. High risk of data loss from local disk failure and manual file transfers.

After Cloud (IaaS & PaaS):

AWS S3 integrated storage with client access portal, paired with automated daily server image backup policies.

99.99% Availability

blueprint recovery speed increased from hours to seconds
